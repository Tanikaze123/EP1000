About HTML

HTML is a widely used website-making programing language. It is easy to learn and implement, but also has a very high skill ceiling, allowing people to use this as a way to show their creativity, using it together with CSS.


Here are some of the simple, but useful, HTML styles and formatting

H1 - title

  • H2 - Sub-Heading

  • H3 - Sub-Heading 2

  • P - paragraph

</div>
